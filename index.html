About Zenith

Zenith, a student-held hackathon in India will host it's second edition this March. We have invited a legion of over 300 hackers over the past one years to collaborate, create and bring to life an idea. The regions we come from are known for their diversity, scenic beauty and some of the best colleges in India but sadly, not for hackathons. That's where we come in. We aim to inculcate innovative development and provide a medium through which bright minds from everywhere can channel out their true potential.

Are you a coder, technocrat, entrepreneur or a creativity digger looking for a challenge? This is an event for coders who have the hacking spirits and love giving life to their ideas.
The goal? You can hack anything that provides a solution to any impactful problem. Channel your focus, build a mobile application or web application or an IOT or any other stuff and show us your project. Anything that can limit you is your own creativity. Make your disruptive ideas happen.
It is a chance to show your metal and show the innovation at its best

APPLICATION PROCEDURE
1.Fill the registration form,a team can have minimum of 1 member and maximum of 3 members. 2.After filling of forms, our team will shortlist your idea. 3.After shorlisting you have to develop your idea and submit screeenshot,video of idea. 4.You will be called to pitch your idea on the day of event.

    Well you are free to choose any kind of idea, any kind of platform. You should write about your idea.How your idea will solve some sort of problem.how your idea is scalable or impactful,how feasible is your project You don't need to write about yourself or your teams, we will review your profiles to know more about you.

  • 3.How will I know I am shortlisted or not??

    As soon as you will register yourself, our shortlisting panel will reply you back within 3-4 days maximum via call/sms/email, to instruct you about further procedures and an official invite will be delivered to you for joining us at onsite project pitching cum demonstration round.

  • 5.What if i am not able to complete the project till the presentation day or what if my team is unable to join inperson at the hackathon site?

    Well in this case, we would request you to contact us as soon as possible, so that we can cancel your slot and give your slot to the next deserving team.
