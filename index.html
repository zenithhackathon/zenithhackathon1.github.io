About Hackadrone

If you are a team of drone-geeks willing to build innovative solutions, here's the launch pad that you’ve been waiting for!

Cyient Ltd and DJI invite you to participate in Hackadrone 2018—India’s first UAV hackathon, sponsored by Microsoft and the Telangana State Government.

We are calling on all developers to create innovative commercial and industrial solutions on the DJI industrial drone platform. Using DJI’s powerful Software Development Kit (SDK), you will demonstrate the value of your concept and its potential to redefine industry practices.

This hackathon is NOT about building DIY (do-it-yourself) drones. Multiple drone platforms for your use and expert help on SDK will be available during the hackathon.

Hackadrone 2018 is open to start-ups, independent software programmers, developers, senior IT professionals, and technology students.

FAQ
  • 1. How can I register for the challenge?
    Register as a new user, by clicking the "Register" button in the portal. Registration is mandatory for participating in the program.
  • 3. What should the team size be?
    Each team can have a minimum of 2 and maximum of 3 members.
  • 5. Can we submit our solutions for more than one theme?
    No, you can only submit solutions for one theme.
  • 7. Who will own the IP generated as part of Hackadrone?
    You will.
  • 9. When and where will the winners be declared?
    Winners will be announced at the end of the event, which will be held on February 9th, 2018 at the Cyient campus in Hyderabad.
  • 2. What is the eligibility for participation in Hackadrone?
    This hackathon is open to start-ups, independent software programmers, developers, senior IT professionals, technology students or anyone else interested in drone development and programming.
  • 4. Can there be individual participation?
    No, you cannot register as an individual.
  • 6. Can we submit multiple solutions for one theme?
    No, you are allowed to submit only one idea per theme.
  • 8. Where will the demo event be conducted?
    The final event will be held at the Cyient campus in Hyderabad, Feb 5-9, 2018.
  • 10. Will the organizers take care of travel and accommodation for the final offline event?
    Yes, accommodation, boarding, lodging, and travel expenses for the invited teams will be borne by Cyient.
